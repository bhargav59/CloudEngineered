Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and handle payments securely through Stripe.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all subscription plans. For reports and consulting services, refunds are available if you're not satisfied within 7 days.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
